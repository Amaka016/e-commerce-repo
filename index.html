Liquor and More


Welcome to CHI&CO wines, Nigeria's premier online liquor store

From big wins to quiet nights in, life is full of moments worth celebrating and at CAhi&co, we make sure you have the perfect bottle for every occasion. Whether you're hosting a party, sending a gift, or simply unwinding after a long day, no need to drive accross town. With just a few clicks, your favorite drinks are on their way, delivered safely, and discreetly to your doorstep.

Our collection is carefully curated with both casual drinkers and connoisseurs in mind. From smooth single-malt whiskies to bold red wines and crisp champagnes, every item in our store is 100% authentic and sourced from trusted distributors.


Shop Exclusive Champagne & Sparkling Wines

Celebrate in style with our luxury range of champagne and bubbly. Perfect for birthdays, weddings, holidays, or simply treating yourself.

Discover the Best in Whisky

Discover our collection of premium whiskies from around the world. From smooth blends to bold single malts, we offer exceptional choices for connoisseurs and casual sippers alike.

Premium Vodka & Craft Spirits

Our vodka selection features top-rated brands known for their clarity, smoothness, and versatility. Ideal for cocktails or sipping neat.

Fine Wines for Every Occasion

Browse our red, white, rose, and dessert wines sourced from award-winning vineyards. Whether you're pairing with a meal or unwinding at night, there's a wine for every moment.

Chilled Bears & Mixers

Stock up on your favorite local and international beers. Need a mixer for your cocktails? We've got tonic, water, soda, juice and more to keep the good times flowing.

Bar Tools & Accessories

Complete your home bar with essential tools and accessories. From royal reserve whiskey decanter set to wine openers and glassware, we've got everything you need to serve drinks like a pro.

Fast & Reliable Delivery

Enjoy swift delivery services accross Nigeria. Whether you're in Lagos, Abuja, or any other major city, we ensure your orders reach you promptly and safely. For those in Lagos, we offer a convenient local pickup option @ Matis Brass, 27 Castle Rock Avenue, Lekki, Lagos Nigeria.

Secure & Flexible Payment Options

Shop with confidence using our secure payment gateways. We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and cash on delivery, providing you with a seamless shopping experience.
