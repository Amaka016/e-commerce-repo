Our Story

CHI&CO is a liquor store in the heart of Owerri, Imo state, Nigeria, looking to be positioned as a go-to brand for her clients to think of when they need seamless customer service getting their favorite liquors and liquor accessories. Hence, CHI&CO meaning liquor and more.

Purchasing drinks from our online store is one of the easiest and seamless ways to shop for all your drinks and drinks accessories as CHI&CO is the largest online distributor of cognac, spirit, wine and accessories in Nigeria.

Established in 2025, our convenient, fast, and reliable service, has contributed to us becoming the fastest-growing drinks supplier throughout Nigeria.

You can simply place a call through to us to place your order. Cheers!
